The Wedding of

Mr. and Mrs. Dunlap

Our Story

It all began on August 28, 2024, at Salisbury University—on a day filled with nerves, new beginnings, and quiet hope for what the future might hold. We were strangers sitting side by side in the same orientation group, both carrying our own stories and challenges, unsure of what this new chapter would bring. Neither of us could have imagined that, in that very moment, we were meeting the person who would change everything.

It took a little nudge from a friendly introduction (thanks to a classmate named Omar) for our story to begin. What started as a simple conversation quickly grew into friendships —day by day— into a whole circle of friends, spending every day together. Somewhere in the middle of all the laughter, introductions, and shared moments, something quietly special began forming between us.

In those early days, it was the smallest gestures that meant the most. Matt noticed when Grace didn’t have enough meal swipes and, without hesitation, began bringing her muffins in the mornings before orientation. Around that same time, when life felt uncertain and overwhelming, he was there for her—offering comfort, patience, and a kind of understanding that felt rare and steady. Long before we ever said how we felt, he showed it in every thoughtful thing he did. It was kindness like that—gentle, unspoken, and genuine—that built the foundation of everything to come.

As the weeks passed, our friend group became inseparable. We spent evenings in the dorm lounge, studying under the glow of a projector, laughing over games of pool, and wandering campus in search of memories to be made. Those cozy nights became our safe place. And somewhere between shared homework, quiet conversations, and lingering glances, our friends began to notice what we hadn’t fully realized yet.

Between teasing smiles and knowing glances, they insisted there was something more between us. They teased us endlessly, going as far as writing a silly little “fanfiction” about us, convinced that something more was there. At first, we laughed it off, but eventually, we couldn’t ignore what was growing in our hearts. What started as friendship slowly, beautifully, turned into more.

One night changed everything in a quiet, unforgettable way. We stayed up far too late watching a horror movie in the lounge, talking and laughing into the early morning hours. Knowing we had classes the next day, but not caring. From that point on, we found ourselves drawn to each other more and more, choosing to spend time together, just the two of us—finding comfort, understanding, and strength in one another during a time when we both needed it most. Through shared struggles and quiet support, we became unbreakable.

After days of spending time together, we began watching the Twilight series—one of the first things we truly shared (Matt’s first time seeing it). We stretched it out over several nights, savoring every moment, and even started The Lord of the Rings together (a journey we wouldn’t finish until the week before our engagement because SOMEONE wanted to watch the extended versions *cough* Matt…). When Twilight finally came to an end, something else was ready to begin.

On September 11, 2024, we made it official.

From that moment on, it felt like everything in life had led us exactly where we were meant to be: together.

Just as Matt was loving Grace, Grace was quietly teaching him something just as important—how to receive it. She showed him that love was never meant to be carried by one person alone, that a relationship is built by two people choosing each other equally, every day. With patience and care, she helped him unlearn the idea that he had to earn love through constant effort or sacrifice. Instead, she reminded him that he was worthy of love simply for who he was—not just in how he cared for her, but in his passions, his values, and the people he held close. In gentle but powerful ways, she helped him reconnect with parts of himself that had once been pushed aside, showing him that love should never isolate or diminish, but instead uplift and strengthen. It was a kind of love that felt entirely new—balanced, freeing, and whole—and together, they began to redefine what a healthy, lasting partnership truly looks like.

One of our favorite places on campus, a beautiful little spot called the “Secret Garden.” Surrounded by flowers, greenery, and a small fountain topped with a pineapple, it became our escape from the world. A place where time slowed down, and it was just us. It was there, in that quiet, beautiful space, that we said, through panicked hearts, “I love you” for the very first time.

We finished our freshman year side by side. Eventually Grace moved into Matts dorm when one of his roomates graduated—just across the hall from each other—and continued building a life that felt more and more shared with each passing day. As time went on, we knew we were ready for a new chapter. Together, we made the decision to move to Arkansas, choosing growth, change, and a future we could build hand in hand.

Life brought its challenges, but through every twist and turn, we chose each other—again and again.

From the very beginning, we both believed in loving with intention—that when you choose someone, you choose them for life, #itgoesbothways. Even then, we couldn’t have known just how quickly our hearts would find home in one another.

On March 14 2026, we got engaged.


Now, here we are—ready to begin forever, together.

With love, Matthias & Gracelynn
