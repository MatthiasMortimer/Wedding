Theme

Hobbitcore

Warm countryside, rustic elegance, and the cozy feeling of a Hobbit celebration—woodland tones, simple beauty, and joyful gathering under the open sky. Think Bilbo's Birthday Party.

Key Elements

  • Materials: Natural fibers like linen, cotton, wool, and tweed
  • Colors: Earthy tones like moss green, brown, cream, ocher, and mustard
  • Key Pieces: Waistcoats, blazers, knickerbockers, suspenders, cloaks
  • Feminine Style: Calf-length skirts, peasant blouses, aprons, bodices, shawls
  • Accessories: Leather belts, floral patterns, straw hats
  • Footwear: Barefoot (optional), moccasins, clogs, or simple boots

Important Notes

  • This page describes the theme, not required clothing items.
  • While we embrace a Shire/medieval fantasy theme, cosplaying is not allowed. Please avoid elf ears, costumes, or character attire.
  • For purposes, please include all household family members. No additional plus-ones unless specifically authorized by the couple
  • We’re going for a hobbit-inspired / cozy medieval fantasy feel—think natural fabrics, soft layers, earthy tones, and relaxed silhouettes.
