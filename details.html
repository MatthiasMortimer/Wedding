Theme

Hobbitcore

Warm countryside, rustic elegance, and the cozy feeling of a Hobbit celebration—woodland tones, simple beauty, and joyful gathering under the open sky. Think Bilbo's Birthday Party.

Key Elements

  • Materials: Natural fibers like linen, cotton, wool, and tweed
  • Colors: Earthy and warm tones such as moss green, brown, cream, ocher, and mustard.
  • Keypieces: Waistcoats (vests), blazers, knickerbockers (cropped pants), suspenders, and cloaks.
  • Feminine Style: Calf-length skirts, peasant blouses, aprons, bodices, and shawls.
  • Accessories: Simple leather belts, ditsy floral patterns, and straw sun hats.
  • Footwear: Barefoot is allowed optionally. Practical options include leather moccasins, clogs, or simple boots.

Important Note

While we embrace a Shire / medieval fantasy theme, cosplaying is not allowed. Please do not wear elf ears, costumes, or character attire. We kindly ask guests to keep attire semi-formal and hobbitcore
